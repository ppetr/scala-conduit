GenPipe

sealed trait GenPipe[-U, -I, +O, +R] extends AnyRef

Accepts input elements of type I and when its upstream has no more output, receives its result U; produces output elements of type O and when finished, returns R.

In most cases, using conduit.Pipe is sufficient over this more general trait.

All operations on GenPipes are defined in the Pipe object.
